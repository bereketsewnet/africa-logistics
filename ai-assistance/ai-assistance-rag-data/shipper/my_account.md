# My Account

## What This Page Is

The My Account page is the shipper's personal profile area.

It is used for identity, security, contact information, and account settings.

## Main Purpose

This page helps the shipper manage their own account information without touching driver-only workflows or admin-only workflows.

## What the Shipper Sees

- Profile photo
- Full name
- Role badge
- Phone number
- Email address and verification state
- Profile information
- Security settings
- Contact settings
- Preferences

## Important Difference From Driver Accounts

Shippers use the same account page layout, but they do not see driver-only tabs such as:

- Driver documents
- Driver rating

Those sections are only shown to drivers.

## Step-by-Step Actions

### 1. Open My Account
- The shipper clicks **My Account** in the sidebar or mobile dock.
- The page opens with the profile header at the top.

### 2. Review personal information
- The shipper can see their name, phone, email, and role.
- If email is verified, the page shows a verified indicator.
- If email is not linked, the page shows that it is missing.

### 3. Update profile photo
- The shipper clicks the small camera button on the avatar.
- They choose an image file.
- After upload, the new photo appears in the profile header.
- They can also remove the photo if needed.

### 4. Change display name
- The shipper opens the Profile tab.
- They edit first name and last name.
- They save the new name.

### 5. Change password
- The shipper opens the Security tab.
- They choose Change Password.
- They enter current password, new password, and confirmation.
- If successful, the password updates immediately.

### 6. Link or change email
- The shipper opens the Contact tab.
- They enter a new email address.
- The system sends a verification link.
- The shipper completes verification from the email inbox.

### 7. Change phone number
- The shipper opens the Contact tab.
- They enter a new phone number.
- The system sends an OTP.
- The shipper enters the OTP to confirm the change.

### 8. Set display theme
- The shipper opens Preferences.
- They can switch between Light, Dark, or System theme.

### 9. Control notifications
- The shipper opens Preferences.
- They can toggle SMS, email, browser, Telegram, order updates, and promotions.

## Important Rules For Bemnet

- This page is for the shipper's own account only.
- Do not describe driver verification tabs as if they are available to shippers.
- Do not describe admin-only identity actions here.
- If the user asks about another user's account, redirect to support or the staff team.
