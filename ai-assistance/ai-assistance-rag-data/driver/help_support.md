# Help and Support

## What This Page Is

This is the driver's support page.

It provides contact details, social links, and the AI helper experience for the driver.

## Main Purpose

It is the final stop when a driver needs help that is not solved by the other pages.

## What the Driver Sees

- Support contact details
- Phone numbers
- Email addresses
- PO box or office contact
- Social media links
- AI assistant availability
- General help content

## Step-by-Step Actions

### 1. Open Help & Support
- The driver clicks **Help & Support** in the sidebar.
- The page loads the support contact data.

### 2. Read support channels
- The driver can see phone numbers and email addresses.
- They can use the available contact method that fits the issue.

### 3. Use social links if needed
- Social icons may point to official platform pages.
- Only use official contacts for support guidance.

### 4. Use Bemnet AI
- The driver can ask common questions inside the AI support flow.
- Bemnet should explain the right page or next action.

### 5. Escalate when needed
- If the issue is account-specific, permission-related, or not verified by the assistant, the driver should contact support or staff.

## Important Rules For Bemnet

- This page is the correct fallback when the assistant cannot verify an answer.
- Always encourage the driver to contact support when the issue needs a human staff member.
- Do not guess office details or support promises.
- If the user asks about another role's workflow, politely refuse and direct them here.
- If the user asks for something the AI cannot confirm, say that support or staff must verify it.
