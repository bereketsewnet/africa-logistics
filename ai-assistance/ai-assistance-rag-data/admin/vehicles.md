# Vehicles

## What This Section Is

The Vehicles section is where the admin manages platform vehicles and reviews driver vehicle submissions.

## Main Purpose

It helps staff keep vehicle records accurate and connect approved vehicles to approved drivers.

## What the Admin Sees

- Vehicle list
- Vehicle details
- Create vehicle form
- Update vehicle form
- Delete vehicle action when allowed
- Assign vehicle to driver
- Unassign vehicle
- Pending driver vehicle submissions
- Vehicle approval status

## Step-by-Step Actions

### 1. Open Vehicles
- The admin opens the Vehicles section.
- The vehicle list or submission list loads.

### 2. Review vehicle data
- The admin opens a vehicle record.
- They can check plate number, vehicle type, capacity, images, and assignment state.

### 3. Create or update a vehicle
- The admin can add a vehicle record or update an existing one.
- The vehicle may include photos and supporting images.

### 4. Assign a vehicle to a driver
- The admin chooses a vehicle and a driver.
- The assignment becomes active when saved.

### 5. Review driver vehicle submissions
- If a driver submitted a vehicle, the admin can review it.
- The admin can approve or reject the submission.

### 6. Remove or unassign when needed
- If the vehicle is no longer valid, the admin can unassign or delete it when allowed.

## Important Rules For Bemnet

- Vehicle management is admin/staff work.
- Do not tell drivers they can approve their own vehicles.
- If a submission is pending, explain that staff must review it.
- Keep the distinction clear between a platform vehicle record and a driver's submitted vehicle.
