# My Account

## What This Page Is

The My Account page is the driver's personal profile area. It is used for identity, security, contact information, and account settings.

## Main Purpose

This page helps the driver manage their own account information without touching job work, wallet work, or admin actions.

## What the Driver Sees

- Profile photo
- Full name
- Role badge
- Driver status badge if the user is a driver
- Phone number
- Email address and verification state
- Profile information
- Security settings
- Contact settings
- Preferences
- Driver documents
- Driver rating section

## Step-by-Step Actions

### 1. Open My Account
- The driver clicks **My Account** in the left sidebar or mobile dock.
- The page opens with the profile header at the top.

### 2. Review personal information
- The driver can see their name, phone, email, and status.
- If email is verified, the page shows a verified indicator.
- If email is not linked, the page shows that it is missing.

### 3. Update profile photo
- The driver clicks the small camera button on the avatar.
- They choose an image file.
- After upload, the new photo appears in the profile header.
- They can also remove the photo if needed.

### 4. Change display name
- The driver opens the Profile tab.
- They edit first name and last name.
- They save the new name.

### 5. Change password
- The driver opens the Security tab.
- They choose Change Password.
- They enter current password, new password, and confirmation.
- If successful, the password updates immediately.

### 6. Link or change email
- The driver opens the Contact tab.
- They enter a new email address.
- The system sends a verification link.
- The driver completes verification from the email inbox.

### 7. Change phone number
- The driver opens the Contact tab.
- They enter a new phone number.
- The system sends an OTP.
- The driver enters the OTP to confirm the change.

### 8. Set display theme
- The driver opens Preferences.
- They can switch between Light, Dark, or System theme.

### 9. Control notifications
- The driver opens Preferences.
- They can toggle SMS, email, browser, Telegram, order updates, and promotions.

### 10. Read verification progress
- If the driver is not fully verified, the page shows upload progress.
- The driver is directed to the Documents area.

### 11. Upload verification documents
- The driver opens Documents.
- They upload National ID, License, and Libre files.
- The system tracks approval status for each file.

### 12. Review rating
- The driver opens Rating.
- They can see their overall score, total trips, and rating summary.

## Important Rules For Bemnet

- This page is for the driver's own account only.
- Do not answer as if the driver can edit other users.
- Do not describe admin-only identity actions here.
- If the user asks about another user's account, redirect to support or the staff team.
- If a field is missing, explain the likely reason and suggest checking profile or support.
