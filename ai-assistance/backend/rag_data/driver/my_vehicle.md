# My Vehicle

## What This Page Is

The My Vehicle page is the driver's vehicle submission and vehicle status page.

## Main Purpose

It lets a driver submit a vehicle for approval, view the current assigned vehicle, and understand the vehicle verification state.

## What the Driver Sees

- Current vehicle information
- Approval state
- Active status
- Plate number
- Vehicle type
- Capacity
- Vehicle submission form if no approved vehicle is active

## Step-by-Step Actions

### 1. Open My Vehicle
- The driver clicks **My Vehicle** from the sidebar.
- The page opens with the current vehicle summary.

### 2. Review the vehicle status
- The driver checks whether the vehicle is approved, pending, rejected, or inactive.
- The page may show the current vehicle type and approval state.

### 3. Refresh the vehicle data
- The driver clicks Refresh if they expect a recent update.
- The page reloads the current vehicle status.

### 4. Submit a vehicle
- If the driver has no approved vehicle, they click **Submit Vehicle**.
- They fill in vehicle details.
- They submit the vehicle for admin approval.

### 5. Understand approval outcome
- If approved, the vehicle becomes part of the driver profile.
- If rejected, the driver should correct the issue and resubmit if the platform allows it.

### 6. Know the vehicle's role in jobs
- The vehicle must match the job requirements when a driver receives work.
- Vehicle capacity and type are part of the assignment logic.

## Important Rules For Bemnet

- This page is about the driver's own vehicle only.
- Do not describe admin vehicle approval as a driver action.
- Do not tell the driver to edit someone else's vehicle.
- If the driver has no vehicle, explain that they need to submit one for review.
- If the vehicle is missing from jobs, suggest checking approval status or support.
