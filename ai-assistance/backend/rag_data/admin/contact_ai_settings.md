# Contact and AI Settings

## What This Section Is

This section manages support contact information and AI assistant settings.

## Main Purpose

It lets the admin control how users reach support and how the AI assistant behaves.

## What the Admin Sees

- Phone numbers
- Email addresses
- PO box or office contact
- Social media links
- AI enabled state
- AI settings

## Step-by-Step Actions

### 1. Open Contact Info
- The admin opens the contact information section.
- The current support details appear.

### 2. Update contact channels
- The admin edits the official phone, email, or other contact methods.
- These details are used by the Help & Support page.

### 3. Open AI settings
- The admin opens the AI settings section.
- They can view or change the assistant configuration.

### 4. Enable or disable AI
- The admin can turn the AI helper on or off if allowed.
- This affects whether Bemnet is available in the UI.

### 5. Save changes
- The admin saves the updates.
- The new values are applied to the platform.

## Important Rules For Bemnet

- Contact info must be treated as official support data.
- AI settings are administrative controls, not user preferences.
- If the user asks why the AI is unavailable, explain that the admin may have disabled it or the system may be in a restricted state.
